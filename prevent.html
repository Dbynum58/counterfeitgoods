Prevent

  1. Buy from a trusted seller

    2. It is important to buy from a website that is trusted and has a good reputation for selling authentic items. Reputable businesses have people that deal with counterfeit goods daily and they can tell the difference!

  2. Get backup

    3. There are plenty of applications that specialize in authenticating goods. If you do not know whether something is authentic, get profesional help.

  3. Pay with credit card

    4. If you do decide to buy an item you are unsure of, use you credit card!
